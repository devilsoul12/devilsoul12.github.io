Growth Tips
What actually works on Twitter in 2025 — no fluff.
01
Never start with "I"
Tweets that start with "I" feel like diary entries. Nobody cares until they know why they should. Start with the insight, the number, the hook.
❌ "I found an AI tool today that..."
✅ "This free AI tool does in 10 seconds what takes me 2 hours:"
02
No hashtags. Seriously.
Hashtags tank your reach on Twitter now. The algorithm doesn't need them. They make you look like a bot or a beginner. Remove them all.
03
Replies > Original tweets for growth
Replying smartly to big accounts (10k-500k followers) in your niche is the fastest way to get followers. One great reply can send 500 people to your profile.
"This is exactly right, and here's what I'd add from building my own app: [your insight]"
04
The 1-2-3 punch format
Lists in tweet format get 3x more saves and shares. Short lines. Punchy. White space between points makes it scannable on mobile.
3 AI tools I use every single day:

1. Claude — for thinking through problems
2. Perplexity — for quick research
3. v0 — for building UI fast

All free to start.
05
Post between 7-10am IST
Most Indian tech Twitter is active in the morning. US audience is active late night IST. For max reach, post at 8am IST — catches both.
06
Controversy drives engagement
You don't need to be mean — just disagree with popular opinions. "Unpopular opinion:" at the start consistently gets replies because people want to argue.
"Unpopular opinion: Learning to code is a waste of time in 2025. Building with AI gets you further, faster. Fight me."
07
Consistency > Virality
1 tweet per day for 90 days beats 10 tweets one day and nothing for a week. The algorithm rewards accounts that show up daily. Set a 5-minute morning ritual.
